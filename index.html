How much do you know about gymnastics?

By Isabella Sorresso

From ages six to 14, I was a competitive gymnast in artistic gymnastics. However, I never referred to it as "artistic gymnastics," it was just called "gymnastics" to me. I knew that other types of competitive gymnastics existed, but none of the gyms nearby offered training in them, so I went about my life thinking that artistic gymnastics was the "real" gymnastics and never learning the ins and outs of the other versions of the sport that I loved. For a long time I didn't know about the other forms of gymnastics, so I'm here to test your knowledge too.
